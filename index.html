FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know before hiring us.

How long does it take to build an Android app?

+

Timelines depend on complexity. A simple app (5–10 screens) takes 3–4 weeks. A medium app with admin panel takes 6–8 weeks. Complex apps with custom APIs may take 10–14 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline in our proposal.

Do you sign an NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)?

+

Yes, absolutely. We sign NDAs for all Enterprise projects and on request for any project. Your idea, business logic, and project details are always kept strictly confidential.

Will I get the full source code?

+

Yes. For Professional and Enterprise plans, complete source code is handed over on project completion. For Starter plans, source code can be added as an optional add-on. You will have full ownership of the code.

What is your payment structure?

+

We follow a 50/50 model — 50% upfront to start, 50% on final delivery. For larger projects, milestone-based payments are available. We accept bank transfer, UPI, and all major payment methods.

Do you provide post-launch support?

+

Yes. Every plan includes a dedicated support period (1–6 months). During this time we fix bugs, handle Play Store issues, and provide guidance at no extra charge. Extended support packages are also available.

Can you work with clients outside Tamil Nadu / India?

+

Absolutely! We are remote-first and work with clients across India and worldwide via WhatsApp, email, Google Meet, or Zoom — whatever works best for you.

Are the prices inclusive of GST?

+

No. All prices listed are exclusive of GST (18%). The applicable GST will be added to the final invoice as per Indian tax regulations. We provide a proper GST invoice for all projects.
